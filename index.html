Welcome

Sepulchretum is an independent black metal project formed in Belgium. Channeling pain, decay, and unearthly sorrow through raw riffs and haunting atmospheres, Sepulchretum crafts an experience both ritualistic and tormented. Each release is a reflection of personal struggle, the void within, and the spirit of underground metal.

About Sepulchretum

Founded by Lewis Hayden in 2024, Sepulchretum draws influence from depressive, atmospheric, and second-wave black metal. With each track composed from scratch on guitar and expanded into full compositions, Lewis handles all songwriting, recording, and production alone.
